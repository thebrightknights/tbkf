Knight's Iftar — Ramadhan di Satu Meja

15 March 2026 Jakarta
Completed
View on Instagram @brightknights.ina

Kicking off Season 2 in the spirit of Ramadan — the first event brought members and children together for iftar. Beyond the meal: Tebakan Ketupat (Ramadan guessing game), warm conversations, and a donation handover. "Ramadhan di Satu Meja" (Ramadan at One Table) embodied the founding spirit of Rodeo.

  • Iftar dinner shared
  • Tebakan Ketupat — traditional Ramadan game
  • Donation handover
  • Opening event of Season 2
