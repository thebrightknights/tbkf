About Us

Our Vision & Mission

"Aspiring to inspire others by giving back to the community — Home to Rodeo, TreasureKnight, Young Knights, and Charity Concert."

🔭

Vision

To become a space for expression and personal growth for people who are facing financial, educational, and emotional challenges in Indonesia.

Menjadi ruang untuk berekspresi dan tumbuh bagi mereka yang memiliki keterbatasan secara finansial, edukasi, dan kebahagiaan di Indonesia.

🎯

Mission

  • 1 Providing opportunities to develop talents, creativity, and skills.
  • 2 Organizing mentoring and recreational activities to build resilience and self-confidence.
  • 3 Establishing collaborations with external partners to ensure program and resource sustainability.
  • 4 Conducting regular evaluations and continuous improvements to expand impact and effectiveness.
