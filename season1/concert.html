🎵 Charity Concert

TBKF's flagship fundraising event of Season 1, a live classical music charity recital open to the public, with all proceeds directed toward community support and future outreach activities.

Season 1 · Fundraising Event

Tchaikovsky and Rachmaninoff: A Charity Recital

Saturday, January 13th, 2024, 15:00 WIB Balai Resital Kertanegara, Jakarta
Completed
Objective

To raise funds in support of The Bright Knight Foundation's outreach programs through a formal classical music performance, while promoting broader appreciation for classical music among the attending public.

Activity Description

The event was a formal charity recital featuring classical compositions by Bach, Tchaikovsky, and Rachmaninoff, performed live at Balai Resital Kertanegara, Jakarta. The performance delivered a refined and high-quality musical experience for the audience, while simultaneously serving as a meaningful platform for social contribution through charitable fundraising. All proceeds from the event were directed toward supporting The Bright Knight Foundation's ongoing community outreach programs and future Rodeo activities.

Impact

The Charity Concert served as a milestone for The Bright Knight Foundation, demonstrating that creative public events can serve a meaningful social purpose. Funds raised were directed into the Season 1 Rodeo budget, enabling the team to expand its outreach visits, bring more supplies, and reach additional orphanages across Jakarta.
