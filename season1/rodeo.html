Season 1 · Rodeo #1

The First Rodeo

2023 Jakarta
Completed
Objective

To inaugurate The Bright Knight Foundation's community outreach mission and establish a replicable model for direct social engagement with orphaned children in Jakarta.

Activity Description

Rodeo #1 marked the inaugural outreach event of The Bright Knight Foundation. A founding team of members visited a local orphanage in Jakarta, bringing donations and spending meaningful time with the children in residence. The event established the foundational framework and outreach model that would grow into a twelve-event season of community service across Jakarta.

New Activities, New Bonds

2024 Dinas Sosial Rumah Perlindungan Lanjut Usia, Jakarta
Completed
Objective

To extend care and companionship to elderly residents at the Dinas Sosial Rumah Perlindungan Lanjut Usia through meaningful social engagement, fostering intergenerational connection and empathy among TBKF members during the Service Month initiative.

Activity Description

Completed

Al-Andalusia Orphanage Foundation: Ramadan Iftar Edition

March 15th, 2025, 16:00–19:00 WIB Al-Andalusia Orphanage Foundation 12 members
Completed
Objective

To share joy and meaningful interaction with the children of Al-Andalusia Orphanage Foundation during the holy month of Ramadan, through donation handover, collaborative games, and the shared experience of preparing and breaking fast together.

Activity Description

The activity was attended by 12 Bright Knight members. It commenced with the formal donation handover to the foundation. This was followed by interactive games involving both members and children. Participants then collaborated in preparing takjil, the traditional snacks customarily served for breaking the fast, fostering a sense of teamwork and closeness among all involved. The event concluded with a warm and meaningful communal iftar, shared between members and the children of the foundation.

Parapattan Orphanage: Independence Day Celebration

Sunday, August 16th, 2025, 09:00–12:00 WIB Parapattan Orphanage 23 members
Completed
Objective

To commemorate Indonesia's Independence Day alongside the children of Parapattan Orphanage through traditional festive competitions and collaborative games, reinforcing a sense of national pride and communal celebration.

Activity Description

The activity was attended by 23 Bright Knight members. Organized in conjunction with Indonesia's Independence Day, the event featured traditional lomba competitions, including the cracker-eating race (makan kerupuk) and the sack race (lompat karung). Additional ice-breaking activities were conducted to maintain a lively and celebratory atmosphere throughout the event. The event concluded with a formal donation handover.
